Canine Mortality

Canine mortality data are publicly available at Pegram et al. (2021). In this dataset, there are 29865 observations used to study euthanasia. Our interest is in survival patterns by age and so we remove records where either the date of birth or death is missing or incorrect (we also removed 17 records with missing sex information); this results in 5933 records for analysis.
